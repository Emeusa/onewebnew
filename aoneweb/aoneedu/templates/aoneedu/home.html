IJMB

Interim Joint Matriculation Board (IJMB), is a federal government-approved program in Nigeria that provides an alternative pathway for students seeking admission into tertiary institutions without taking the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) examination.
Benefits: Guaranteed admission into Nigerian universities, flexible payment plans, and expert guidance
